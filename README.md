# Bowling-HQ

**A Personal Bowling Intelligence System** that learns how you bowl and makes increasingly accurate recommendations.

## What It Does

Bowling-HQ answers three questions before, during, and after bowling:
1. **What ball should I throw?** - Intelligent ball selection based on conditions, history, and patterns
2. **Where should I play the lane?** - Lane play recommendations with confidence scores
3. **What should my next move be?** - Real-time transition guidance during sessions

**The system provides answers in less than 5 seconds.**
