# Section 1: Project Overview

## What is Bowling-HQ?

Bowling-HQ is an **AI-powered bowling intelligence platform** that combines digital twin technology, pattern recognition, and real-time analytics to help bowlers of all levels optimize performance, build strategic equipment lineups, and make data-driven decisions.

## The Vision

Transform bowling from intuition-based to **data-driven and AI-assisted**. Give every bowler access to insights that were previously only available to professionals and coaches.

---

## Core Features

### 1. **Ghost Bowler** (Digital Twin)
Your AI-powered bowling persona that learns from your bowling history and predicts how you'll bowl in new conditions.

- Tracks bowling style evolution
- Predicts performance in unfamiliar lanes
- Suggests adjustments for success
- Learns from every game played

### 2. **Commander AI** (Decision Engine)
Real-time recommendations for ball selection and strategy during league play or tournaments.

- Analyzes current lane conditions
- Recommends optimal ball from your arsenal
- Suggests strategy adjustments
- Provides confidence ratings

### 3. **Arsenal DNA** (Equipment Database)
Comprehensive knowledge system about bowling equipment.

- Ball specifications and characteristics
- Layout analysis and matching
- Performance comparisons
- Recommendation engine

### 4. **Pattern Intelligence** (Lane Analysis)
Advanced understanding of lane conditions and how they affect ball performance.

- Lane pattern recognition
- Oil distribution analysis
- Pattern difficulty scoring
- Historical pattern database

### 5. **Tournament Bag** (Lineup Builder)
Strategic tool for building the perfect equipment lineup.

- Optimal ball combinations
- Coverage analysis
- Contingency strategies
- Equipment rotation suggestions

### 6. **Session Intelligence** (Analytics)
Track and analyze every bowling session.

- Performance metrics
- Trend analysis
- Comparative analytics
- Session-to-session improvements

### 7. **Maxwell AI** (Venue Intelligence)
Information about bowling centers and their characteristics.

- Lane condition history
- Center-specific patterns
- Seasonal trends
- Performance benchmarks

### 8. **Galaxy** (Arsenal Visualization)
Interactive 3D visualization of your bowling ball arsenal.

- Ball performance comparison
- Coverage mapping
- Inventory management
- Visual strategy planning

### 9. **Video Coach** (Analysis Tool)
AI analysis of bowling videos.

- Form feedback
- Release analysis
- Approach suggestions
- Comparison to optimal form

### 10. **Digital Twin Predictions** (Predictive AI)
Advanced predictive models for future performance.

- Tournament outcome predictions
- Scoring forecasts
- Equipment performance under conditions
- Long-term trend analysis

### 11. **Lane Simulator** (Strategy Engine)
Simulate different strategies and see predicted outcomes.

- Test equipment combinations
- Simulate lane conditions
- Predict score outcomes
- Risk analysis

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python), PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis
- **Frontend**: React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **AI/ML**: TensorFlow, Scikit-learn
- **Infrastructure**: Docker, AWS

---

## Data-Driven Approach

Every feature is built on real bowling data:

- **Historical Performance**: Your complete bowling history
- **Lane Patterns**: Thousands of documented lane conditions
- **Equipment Data**: Extensive ball specification database
- **Center Information**: Venue-specific characteristics
- **Statistical Models**: Proven bowling analytics
