# Section 12: Academy - Learning & Skill Development

## What is Academy?

**Academy** is your comprehensive bowling education platform. It provides structured learning paths, skill development programs, coaching videos, drills, technique tutorials, and expert instruction to help you improve from beginner to advanced levels.

Think of it as your **personal bowling university**—offering:
- Skill-building courses
- Expert coaching videos
- Progressive drill programs
- Technique mastery
- Tournament preparation
- Specialized training
