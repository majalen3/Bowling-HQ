# Section 10: Video Coach - AI Analysis & Feedback

## What is Video Coach?

**Video Coach** is your AI-powered video analysis system. Upload bowling videos, and Video Coach analyzes your technique, provides feedback, identifies issues, suggests corrections, and tracks improvement over time.

Think of it as having a **personal bowling coach on call**—providing:
- Technical analysis
- Real-time feedback
- Improvement suggestions
- Form correction
- Consistency tracking
- Progress visualization
